Tired of the routine?

When you sing, musical vibrations move through your body, altering your physical and emotional makeup.

Not just in chocolate!

Studies show that singing produces endorphins, the same substance generated when we exercise or eat chocolate. In addition to having a powerful analgesic action, this hormone also stimulates the feeling of well-being, self-confidence, optimism and comfort.

Why are you so angry?

Singing regularly significantly reduces cortisol levels, indicating a reduction in stress.
